# Updated Personas

## Project Sponsor
The Project Sponsor is responsible for ensuring that the project aligns with the organization's strategic goals. They provide high-level guidance and support and are the primary point of contact for executive stakeholders. The Project Sponsor works closely with the Project Manager to facilitate resources and resolve conflicts that may arise during the project lifecycle.

## Release Coordinator
The Release Coordinator manages the release schedule and ensures that all necessary preparations are completed for a successful deployment. This role involves coordinating with various teams, such as development, QA, and operations, to confirm readiness and address any issues that may affect the release timeline.

## QA Lead
The QA Lead is responsible for setting the quality assurance strategy and ensuring that the project meets the defined quality standards. They lead testing efforts, coordinate with developers to resolve bugs, and provide feedback on product functionality to enhance user experience. The QA Lead works alongside the Project Manager and Release Coordinator to ensure quality is maintained throughout the project.

## Change Champion
The Change Champion advocates for change management practices within the project. They work to promote acceptance of change among stakeholders, ensure communication of changes is clear and effective, and support training and resource availability for team members affected by changes. The Change Champion collaborates with the Project Sponsor and Project Manager to align project changes with organizational strategy.
